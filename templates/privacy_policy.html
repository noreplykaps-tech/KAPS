Other information: If you use our website, we may collect information about your device, IP address, and the browser you're using. We might also collect what type of mobile device you are using, or the version of the operating system your computer or device is running.

We collect information directly from you: We collect information directly from you when you sign up or request a test drive on the Site. We also collect information if you post a comment on our Site, request a callback, or ask us a question through phone or email.

We use information to send you transactional communications: We might send you emails or SMS about your account, any test drives you may request/undertake, any car purchase you may make or any car you may request to sell.
